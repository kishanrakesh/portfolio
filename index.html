About Me

I'm Kishan Rakesh, a second-year Master's student in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Dallas, specializing in software engineering, distributed systems, and accessibility-focused technologies.

I deeply value collaborative, innovative environments and am passionate about creating scalable, user-focused solutions with meaningful impact. Upon graduating in May 2026, I aim to contribute to ambitious engineering projects alongside teams committed to technological excellence and solving global-scale problems.
